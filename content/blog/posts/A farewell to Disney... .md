Today is a sad day for us as we have to leave Disney tonight and travel home in the morning. 

A quick rundown of today's events.. 
Saw 'Together' once again. I'm never going to get sick of this show. 

Next up Spider-man again which was my chance of redemption, but alas no luck. 

We then did the Ratatouille ride again as we then waved by to the Disney Studios park. We'll do the big rides in June 

In the main park we saw Mickeys PhilharMagic show and then rode Star Tours one final time. Luckily it was a different trip this time on Jakku followed by a trip to Exegol to help Finn. 

Then we ate at the silver spoon I had tomato soup, followed by steak and then a sundae.
